Recruiting PhD Students!

KAWAII lab @ HKU is always looking for passionate PhD students. If you are interested, please send your

  • CV describing your publication history and research statement, and
  • Research statement describing your research plan based on literature review,

to our Group Leader, Professor SUZUKI, Atsushi via email at atsushi.suzuki.rd [at] outlook.com. Note: we will get back to suitable candidates only.

For information on admission requirements, application procedures, and scholarships, please refer to the Postgraduate Admission page of the Department of Mathematics, HKU.
