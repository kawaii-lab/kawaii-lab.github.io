Recruiting PhD Students!

KAWAII AI lab @ HKU is always looking for passionate PhD students. If you are interested, please send your

  • CV describing your publication history and other achievements,
  • Research statement describing your research plan based on literature review, and
  • Hong Kong PhD Fellowship Scheme (HKPFS) application draft if you wish to receive a scholarship,
  • GitHub URL to show your IT/programming experiences,

to our Group Leader, Professor SUZUKI, Atsushi via email at atsushi.suzuki.rd [at] outlook.com. Note: we will get back to suitable candidates only.

Next deadline to contact the Group Leader: 2025-10-17 (for the main round in 2025-26)

Applicants who wish to receive a scholarship must apply for Hong Kong PhD Fellowship Scheme (HKPFS). Several scholarship schemes exist apart from the HKPFS; however, they are all exclusively for applicants who have applied for, but were not selected for, the HKPFS.

For information on other admission requirements, application procedures, and scholarships, please refer to the HKU Graduate School website (How to Apply? - Admission Requirement - Application Period - Supporting Documents - FAQ on Application).

Please also refer to the following webpages:

  • Join US - Studying RPg at HKU Science, the Faculty of Science, HKU
  • The Postgraduate Admission page of the Department of Mathematics, HKU

Note: We are not recruiting students for the MPhil course currently.
